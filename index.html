About Vivek Singh Chandel

Vivek Singh Chandel is a highly accomplished Research Engineer and Technology Consultant with over 14+ years of experience in IT operations, advanced technology solutions, and business management. With deep expertise in cutting-edge fields such as Artificial Intelligence (AI), Internet of Things (IoT), Blockchain, Cloud Computing, and Machine Learning (ML), Vivek has contributed to numerous large-scale projects across diverse industries including healthcare, defense, and industrial automation.

As a visionary leader in technology, Vivek has led projects like the IoT-based VARGO health telemetry platform, AI-powered operational improvement systems, 5G network infrastructures, and decentralized blockchain platforms. His in-depth knowledge extends to areas like big data analytics, predictive modeling, edge computing, and decentralized systems, showcasing his ability to merge research with real-world applications.

Notable Projects

Integrated predictive analytics and edge computing for immediate health insights.
